Biography:

Amida Crew was founded in 2012 and competed for the first time at Rockers Rumble. The original members: Terry, Steve, Quang, Sui, and Yuta are still active members of Amida. Over the years Amida has expanded and reached out to dancers such: Hogon, Peter, Nikolai, Julio, Sarina, Sarah, Deco, and Yosh. We are a group that focuses on breaking, but we've realize that by getting involved with other dance events outside the breaking scene, it has helped us grow and understand the community in a much higher level.

On June 12, 2016 we lost Sarah and Peter to a car accident. They will always be a part of Amida and what it represents. We dance in memory of them, and we love them every day. Rest In Beats Ball and Peter.
